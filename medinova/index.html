Emergency SOS Service

In case of medical emergency, our SOS feature instantly connects you to the nearest hospital and emergency services. Help is just one click away.

How Emergency SOS Works

  • 1
    Press SOS Button

    Tap the emergency SOS button to activate the emergency protocol instantly.

  • 2
    Location Detection

    We automatically detect your location and find the nearest hospital.

  • 3
    Emergency Contact

    We notify emergency services and the nearest hospital emergency department.

  • 4
    Medical Assistance

    Emergency medical teams receive your medical history and head to your location.

Press and hold the SOS button in case of medical emergency. This will immediately alert emergency services and the nearest hospital.

⚠ Important Notice

This is a demonstration feature. In a real emergency, please call your local emergency number (911, 112, etc.) immediately.
